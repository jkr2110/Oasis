Oasis for Business

The Oasis for Business map is a resource for existing and prospective business owners to understand the food resources in their area. The map includes information about zoning and tax incentive regions under the NYC FRESH program, existing grocery stores and bodegas in the area, and the population density by community district.

Fresh Food Availability Index (FFA)

The FFA map shows the calculated FFA across the city and can be compared and contrasted with demographic information such as population and median household income. As the cornerstone of our project, we hope that the FFA map will expose the inequality that exists in New York City when it comes to access to healthy and fresh food.
